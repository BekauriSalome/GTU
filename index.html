Doownload Certificat

Automated sorting of recycled materials is crucial for efficient waste management and recycling processes. NERF-BASED SCENE PERCEPTION FOR AN INDUSTRIAL DELTA PARALLEL ROBOT

Doownload Certificat

This paper presents an ambitious approach to scene perception for industrial setups utilizing Neural Radiance Fields (NeRFs) with a focus on a delta parallel robot. Traditional methods such as laser scanners, infrared projectors, and multi-camera stereo methods have limitations, especially in scenarios with diverse materials or dynamic scenes.
